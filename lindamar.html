Lindamar Beach

Pacifica's mellow beach break — the best place to learn near San Francisco

Back to Home

Lindamar (also called Pacifica State Beach) is the go-to spot for anyone learning to surf near San Francisco. Located in Pacifica, about 10 miles south of the city, it offers a more forgiving beach break than Ocean Beach, with smaller and more consistent waves that are perfect for beginners.

Multiple surf shops are located right next to the beach — you can walk in, rent a board and wetsuit, take a lesson, and be in the water within the hour. It's the most accessible entry point to surfing in the Bay Area.

Getting There

Take Highway 1 south from San Francisco through Daly City and into Pacifica. Look for Beach Boulevard in Pacifica and follow it to the ocean. Pacifica State Beach is well-signed. There's a paid parking lot right at the sand — expect to pay a few dollars on weekends.

Alternatively, you can take SamTrans bus route 112 from BART's Daly City station directly to Pacifica.
