For Beginners

Learning To Surf Around San Francisco

Learning to surf is fun, but having the right surf conditions, equipment, and instructors make all the difference. If you're a beginner looking to surf around San Francisco, the two best places are Lindamar beach in Pacifica and Bolinas in Marin.

Both beaches have surf shops nearby where you can rent wetsuits, booties, and surfboards. There are also several great spots for lessons with experienced instructors who know these waters well.

The water up here is cold year-round (50–60°F), so a good wetsuit isn't optional — it's essential. Below is your guide to getting started.
