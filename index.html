Learning To Surf Around SF

Learning to surf is fun, but having the right conditions, equipment, and instructors make all the difference. The two best beginner spots near San Francisco are Lindamar in Pacifica and Bolinas in Marin.

The water is cold year-round (50–60°F), so a good wetsuit isn't optional — it's essential.
