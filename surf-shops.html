Where to gear up, rent boards, and get local intel

The Bay Area has a solid handful of core surf shops that have been serving the local community for years. Whether you need to rent for your first lesson or buy a new stick, these are the places to go.

San Francisco

Aqua Surf Shop

One of the original SF surf shops, located right near Ocean Beach. Full selection of boards, wetsuits, and accessories.

aquasurfshop.com →

Mollusk Surf Shop

A beloved SF institution in the Inner Sunset. Known for a curated board selection and a strong community ethos.

mollusksurfshop.com →

Pacifica

NorCal Surf Shop

Right at Lindamar beach — rentals, lessons, and repairs. Perfect starting point for beginners.

norcalsurfshop.com →

Sonlight Surf Shop

A longtime Pacifica staple. Good selection of longboards and wetsuits for the colder Bay Area waters.

sonlightsurfshop.com →

Half Moon Bay

On The Beach Surf Shop

Serving the Half Moon Bay and Mavericks area. Great local knowledge on the coastside breaks.

onthebeachsurfshop.com →

Marin

Nor Cal Surf & Skate

Based in the North Bay, serving the Stinson Beach and Bolinas crowd.
